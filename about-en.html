About Us

Research platform on Georgia's integration process with the European Union

Our Mission

evropa.eu.org is a research platform that aims to present Georgia's European integration process and the impact of disinformation in an accessible and understandable way for the general public. Our main task is to provide objective information and raise media literacy.

What We Do

We analyze and research the spread of disinformation in digital media, social networks, and Georgian websites. We study the evolution of media and public opinion.

Who We Work For

Our audience includes all Georgian citizens, students, journalists, researchers, and anyone interested in Georgia's European future and objective information.

What We Offer

Media History

The development of Georgian media from 1925 to the digital era - how we got from radio to digital media.

Disinformation Analysis

How fake news spreads, Sputnik's narratives, and Russian "soft power" against European integration.

Public Opinion

Public opinion research, ethnic minority attitudes, and media literacy statistics.

Educational Purpose

This platform is created for educational and informational purposes. Our goal is to raise the level of media literacy in society and promote the development of critical thinking.
