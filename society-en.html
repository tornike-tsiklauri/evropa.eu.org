How protected is the Georgian voter from disinformation?

Public Opinion

Positive Attitude
54%
Neutral / No Answer

Note: The neutral segment (44%) is most vulnerable to disinformation, as they have not yet formed a firm position.

Media Literacy Problem

According to the Bulgaria Open Society Institute Index, Georgia ranks 41st place among 41 countries.

This means that the population has difficulty distinguishing fake news from real news, which increases the risk of disinformation impact.

What is the solution? (Recommendations)

Improve cooperation with Facebook and expand the reach of organizations such as "Myth Detector" and "FactCheck.ge".

Attention should be paid not only to outright lies, but also to disguised propaganda spread under the name of reliable sources (e.g., quoting Western experts out of context).

Raising the level of media literacy is critically important. Training and active involvement of social groups are needed.
